COMPANY SUPPORT POLICY DOCUMENT
================================

BILLING AND PAYMENTS
---------------------
- Bills are generated on the 1st of every month.
- Payment is due within 7 days of bill generation.
- If payment is not received within 7 days, the account is suspended.
- A suspended account can be reactivated by paying the outstanding balance plus a ₹50 reactivation fee.
- Accepted payment methods: UPI, Net Banking, Credit Card, Debit Card, Paytm, PhonePe.
- For billing disputes, raise a ticket within 30 days of bill date.
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

DATA AND PLAN POLICIES
-----------------------
- Data rolls over for Premium and Gold plan customers (not Basic).
- Speed is throttled to 1 Mbps after data limit is exhausted (not suspended).
- Plan upgrades take effect immediately.
- Plan downgrades take effect from the next billing cycle.
- International roaming must be activated separately; call customer care to enable.

ORDER AND DELIVERY POLICIES
-----------------------------
- Standard delivery: 3-5 business days.
- Express delivery: next-day delivery available in metro cities (additional ₹99).
- Free delivery on orders above ₹499.
- Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement for full refund.
- After 2 hours, cancellation is subject to seller approval.
- Returns accepted within 10 days for electronics (must be unopened/unused unless defective).
- Return pickup is scheduled within 48 hours of return request approval.

ACCOUNT SECURITY
-----------------
- Password can be reset via registered email or OTP to registered mobile.
- If account is compromised, call immediately — we will lock the account within 5 minutes.
- Two-factor authentication is recommended and can be enabled in app settings.
- Account details can only be changed by the registered account holder after OTP verification.

ESCALATION POLICY
------------------
- Level 1 (AI Agent): Billing queries, order status, plan info, basic troubleshooting.
- Level 2 (Human Agent): Refund disputes, account compromise, legal notices.
- Level 3 (Senior Manager): Issues unresolved after 72 hours.
- Guaranteed callback within 2 hours for Level 2 escalations.

COMMON TROUBLESHOOTING
------------------------
- Internet not working: Restart device. Check if data is exhausted. Check if account is active. Try toggling airplane mode.
- App not loading: Clear cache, reinstall app, check internet connection.
- OTP not received: Check spam folder. Wait 2 minutes. Request OTP again. Check if phone number is correct.
- Payment failed but money deducted: This is a bank hold — it will auto-reverse in 3-5 business days. If not, raise a ticket with transaction screenshot.
